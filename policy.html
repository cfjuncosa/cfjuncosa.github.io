Policy work

Selected edited volumes, report chapters, and policy papers.

  • Unlocking Growth in a Changing World: Formalization, Innovation, and Integration for Latin America and the Caribbean
    Co-edited volume · with Fernando Alvarez Parra.
    Revisits the main levers to spur growth in the region, organized around three intermediate objectives: reducing informality, fostering innovation, and promoting meaningful integration with the world (the “3 Is” of economic growth).
  • Knowledge that Transforms: 20 Years Serving a Prosperous, Inclusive, and Sustainable Future
    Co-edited volume · with Fernando Alvarez Parra.
    Anniversary volume of CAF’s Report on Economic Development, outlining a comprehensive vision of socioeconomic development and emphasizing the value of producing knowledge from and for the region.
  • Clean Fuels
    Book chapter · CAF Report on Economic Development 2024 · with Walter Cont.
    Examines decarbonization pathways for fuels in hard-to-abate sectors such as aviation and high-temperature industrial processes, and the policies needed to promote their development.
  • Green Electrification
    Book chapter · CAF Report on Economic Development 2024 · with Walter Cont.
    Analyzes the transformations required in the power sector to support the transition to carbon-neutral energy systems.
  • Energy Systems in Latin America and the Caribbean
    Book chapter · CAF Report on Economic Development 2024 · with Walter Cont.
    Describes regional energy systems and quantifies losses across production, transformation, and consumption stages, highlighting efficiency as a key component of decarbonization.
  • Ecosystems and Biodiversity in the Face of Climate Change
    Book chapter · CAF Report on Economic Development 2023 · with Ricardo Estrada.
    Explores the complex relationship between ecosystems and human well-being, and identifies policy options to protect biodiversity in Latin America and the Caribbean.
  • Transport Infrastructure
    Book chapter · CAF Report on Economic Development 2021 · with Lian Allub.
    Discusses transport infrastructure as a determinant of trade costs and outlines policies to improve transport services in support of regional and global value chains.
  • Regional Integration, Physical Connectivity, and Global Value Chains (Integración Regional, Conectividad Física y Cadenas Globales de Valor)
    Policy paper · with Lian Allub and Walter Cont.
    Analyzes regional integration and connectivity as key drivers of participation in global value chains.
  • Spatial Distribution of Formal Employment in the Autonomous City of Buenos Aires (Distribución Espacial del Empleo Formal en la Ciudad Autónoma de Buenos Aires)
    Working paper · with Guillermo Alves, Lucila Berniell, Victoria Castillo, Daniel Fernández, and Sebastián Rotondo.
    Analyzes the spatial distribution of formal employment using administrative records.
